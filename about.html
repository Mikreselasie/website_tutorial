Our Mission

Mikre Code Camp is dedicated to bridging the gap between academic computer science education and industry demands in Ethiopia. We provide intensive, practical programming training that complements the theoretical foundation students receive in Ethiopian universities.

Our curriculum is specifically designed to align with the Computer Science programs offered at leading Ethiopian universities, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for both local and international tech opportunities.

Teaching Methodology

Hands-On Learning

Every concept is reinforced through practical coding exercises and real-world projects.

Project-Based Approach

Students build a portfolio of applications that demonstrate their skills to employers.

Collaborative Environment

Peer programming and group projects mirror real software development workflows.

Industry Mentorship

Regular sessions with working professionals provide career guidance and networking.

Location

Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Conveniently located in the heart of Ethiopia's tech hub, with easy access to major universities and tech companies.
